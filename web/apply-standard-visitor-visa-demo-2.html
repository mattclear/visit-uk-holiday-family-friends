Visit the UK on a Standard Visitor visa

Check what you need to do before you can apply for a Standard Visitor visa and what to do when you arrive in the UK.

  1. Step 1 Check if you need a visa

    1. Check if you need a visa

  2. Step 2 Check if this visa is right for you

    1. Check what you can do on a Standard Visitor visa

    You can check what other visas you might be eligible for if you're coming:

    • for a holiday or to see family or friends
    • for a business trip or training
    • for research
    • to study
    • to get married
    • for 48 hours or less
    • to get private medical treatment

  3. Step 3 Prepare the evidence you need

    1. Check what you need to show evidence of
    2. Check what documents you can use as evidence
    3. Check what to do if you're under 18
    4. If your visa is for more than 6 months, check if you need a TB test

  4. Step 4 Apply

    The cost of your visa depends on how long you're staying in the UK.

    1. Apply for a visa and book an appointment £93 to £798
    2. Check where your visa appointment will be

    You should get a decision within 3 weeks of your appointment.

    You can pay an extra £212 for a decision within 5 working days or £956 for a decision within 24 working hours.

  5. Step 5 Attend an appointment

    1. What you need to show at your appointment

    You'll be told when a decision has been made about your visa.

  6. Step 6 Prepare to travel to the UK

    1. Check what you need to show at the UK border
    2. Check what you can bring with you

    Keep the documents that prove why you're visiting the UK in your hand luggage so you can show them if asked.

    1. Check the documents you can use to show why you're visiting the UK
    2. Bringing food, animals or plants into the UK
    3. Bringing your pet to the UK
    4. Get faster entry through the border

  7. Step 7 Travel to the UK

    1. Find out how your baggage will be checked
    2. Check if you need to register with the police after you arrive
